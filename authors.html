Authors of Kids' Novels

  • Roald Dahl - Author of "Matilda" and "Charlie and the Chocolate Factory."
  • J.K. Rowling - Famous for the "Harry Potter" series loved by kids worldwide.
  • David Walliams- Known for books like "Gangsta Granny" and "Billionaire Boy."
  • Enid Blyton - Creator of the beloved "Famous Five" and "Secret Seven" series.
  • Rick Riordan - Writer of the "Percy Jackson" series, bringing mythology to life for kids.
  • Beatrix Potter - Known for timeless classics like "The Tale of Peter Rabbit."
  • C.S. Lewis - Author of "The Chronicles of Narnia," starting with "The Lion, the Witch and the Wardrobe."
